About Us

Sweet Crumbs Bakery was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to bring joy to our community with the freshest, tastiest, and most beautiful baked goods. What began as a family-run home bakery has grown into a beloved local institution, famous for our creative cakes, melt-in-your-mouth cupcakes, and hearty breads.

We believe in using only premium ingredients—no preservatives, just pure goodness. Every item is prepared fresh daily by our passionate bakers. Whether you’re celebrating a birthday, wedding, festival, or just want a sweet treat, we’re here to make every moment special.

Our Values

  • Quality First: We never compromise on ingredients or taste.
  • Personal Touch: Customization for every order, big or small.
  • Community: Supporting local farmers and engaging in local events.
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging and responsible sourcing.
